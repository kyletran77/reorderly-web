What is a reorder point?

A reorder point (ROP) is the inventory level at which you should place a new order with your supplier. It's calculated to ensure you receive new stock before running out, accounting for your supplier's lead time and a safety buffer.

How do I calculate my average daily sales?

Divide your total units sold over the last 30–90 days by the number of days in that period. For seasonal products, use a shorter recent window to capture current demand trends.

What is a good safety stock level?

Safety stock depends on the variability of your demand and supplier lead times. A simple starting point is 20–30% of your average demand during lead time. Use our Safety Stock Calculator for a more precise number.

How do I automate reorder point monitoring?

Reorderly connects to your Shopify store, tracks inventory levels against your reorder points, and automatically sends PO emails to your suppliers when stock hits the threshold — so you never have to check manually.
