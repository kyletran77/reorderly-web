What is a good inventory health score?

80+ is excellent — your inventory processes are well-managed with minimal stockout risk. 60–79 is good but has specific gaps to address. Below 60 means significant improvements are needed to protect your revenue.

How do I improve my inventory health score?

Focus on: (1) setting data-driven reorder points, (2) calculating safety stock, (3) tracking actual supplier lead times, and (4) automating purchase orders. Each of these areas has a direct impact on your score and your revenue.
