                Frequently asked questions

                What data sources does this use?

                Three sources: (1) the competitor's product page, (2) Amazon customer reviews for that product, and (3) Reddit discussions about the brand and category. Claude synthesizes all three into a structured intelligence report.

                Why are real customer reviews so valuable for ad creative?

                The best ad copy sounds like it was written by the customer, not a marketer. When you use the exact words customers use to describe their pain ("my thighs look like cottage cheese"), their frustrations, and their transformation, ads feel personal and real — and they convert much better than polished marketing language.

                How is this different from ChatGPT?

                ChatGPT can't browse the web in real time. This tool actively scrapes the competitor's page, their Amazon listing, and Reddit — pulling live data. The AI then synthesizes actual customer language, not invented copy. A chatbot would hallucinate this; we pull the real thing.

                How long does it take?

                Usually 20-35 seconds. We scrape three data sources in parallel to keep it fast.

                Yes, completely free. No signup, no credit card.
