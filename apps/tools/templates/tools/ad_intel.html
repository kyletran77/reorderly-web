          Frequently asked questions

          What does the Ad Intelligence Tool do?

          It scrapes a competitor's product page and uses AI to extract their positioning, core claims, target avatars, and proven ad angles. It also generates hook ideas and surfaces content gaps — angles they're NOT running that represent opportunities for you.

          How does it work?

          Paste any product URL. The tool fetches the page, extracts the text content, then runs it through Claude (Anthropic's AI) to analyze the positioning and generate a structured intelligence report. The whole process takes 10-20 seconds.

          Can I see their actual Facebook ads?

          Yes — the report includes a direct link to the Meta Ad Library for that brand, where you can see every active ad they're running, the creative, copy, and how long each ad has been live.

          What types of URLs work best?

          Product detail pages work best — Shopify product pages, Amazon listings, DTC brand product pages. The tool extracts claims, benefits, and positioning language from the page content. The more copy on the page, the richer the analysis.

          Is this free?

          Yes, completely free. No signup, no credit card.
